## Strategic Positioning in the Eastern Mediterranean

Israel, with its strategic location along the Suez Canal corridor and heavy reliance on maritime trade, presents another significant market for Sulnox. The ports of Haifa and Ashdod handle the vast majority of Israel's imports and exports, creating a substantial marine fuel market. Similar to Chile and Peru, Israel has ambitious decarbonization targets, with transport identified as a key area of focus. This creates an opportunity for Sulnox to offer a transitional solution that reduces fuel consumption and emissions in the short term while the country transitions towards electrification and alternative energy sources. The patents also extend Sulnox's reach to neighboring markets like Jordan and Saudi Arabia, where they also hold patents.
